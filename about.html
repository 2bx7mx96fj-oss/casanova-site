Who We Are

More Than Design.
A Movement.

Casa Nova Interiors is a premier interior design, construction, and project delivery company dedicated to transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments.

Through innovation, craftsmanship, and strategic global partnerships, we provide end-to-end solutions that bridge design excellence with practical execution — across luxury residences, corporate offices, hospitality, retail, healthcare, and commercial developments.

Based in Karen, Nairobi, we serve clients across Kenya and beyond — bringing world-class standards to every project we touch.
