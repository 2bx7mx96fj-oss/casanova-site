A Message From Our Founder

Casa Nova was born from
a vision much bigger
than design.

Growing up, I understood that a house is more than walls and a roof. It is a place of safety, belonging, dignity, healing, and hope.

Yet many families dream of decent homes, quality furnishings, and comfortable living spaces that often seem beyond reach. My vision is to build a company that bridges that gap.

Through Casa Nova Interiors, I hope to make quality design, construction, furnishing solutions, and home essentials more accessible to families — while creating opportunities that uplift communities and improve livelihoods.

The dream extends beyond beautiful spaces. I envision a future where Casa Nova Interiors contributes to transforming lives, supporting vulnerable families, creating employment opportunities, empowering young people, and establishing rescue and support centres that provide safety, restoration, and hope for those in need.

"The name Esther carries a powerful meaning. In Scripture, Esther was called for a purpose greater than herself — to stand in the gap and bring deliverance to her people."
The Book of Esther

My prayer is that through this company, I may serve others with that same spirit of purpose, compassion, and impact.

Every project we complete, every partnership we build, and every life we touch brings us one step closer to that vision.

With gratitude and purpose,
Esther Nyambura Ngumi
Founder & Lead Designer — Casa Nova Interiors
