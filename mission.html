Beyond Beautiful Spaces

Building a Legacy
That Lasts.

Our founder Esther Nyambura Ngumi built Casa Nova with a vision that reaches far beyond interior design. The dream has always been to create a company that uplifts — not just the spaces we transform, but the people and communities around them.

Through our work, we aim to create meaningful employment, provide opportunities for young designers and craftspeople, and eventually establish support structures for vulnerable families — giving them access to the dignity of a well-designed home.

"The name Esther carries a powerful meaning. In Scripture, Esther was called for a purpose greater than herself — to stand in the gap and bring deliverance to her people."
The Book of Esther

My prayer is that through this company, we may serve others with that same spirit of purpose, compassion, and lasting impact.
