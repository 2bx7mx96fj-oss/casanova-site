The Process

Our process follows five stages:
  • Consultation — we listen to your vision and assess your space
  • Design Concept — mood boards, 3D layouts and a detailed proposal
  • Sourcing — procuring premium materials and products globally
  • Execution — skilled team transforms your space with precision
  • Handover — a finished, fully styled space delivered to you
Timelines vary depending on the scope of the project:
  • Interior styling / soft furnishings: 2–4 weeks
  • Single room design and fit-out: 4–8 weeks
  • Full home renovation: 3–6 months
  • Large commercial projects: 6–12 months
We provide a detailed timeline at the start of every project so you always know what to expect.
